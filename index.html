About Us

Our Mission

The Safe and Agile Transportation Infrastructure (SATi) Lab at Kennesaw State University is dedicated to advancing transportation safety, efficiency, and sustainability through cutting-edge research and innovative technology solutions.

Research Excellence

Conducting high-impact research to address critical transportation challenges and improve infrastructure resilience.

Education

Training the next generation of transportation engineers and researchers through hands-on experience and mentorship.

Collaboration

Partnering with industry, government, and academic institutions to develop practical solutions for real-world transportation problems.

Our Focus

Research Areas

Exploring innovative solutions for safer, more efficient, and sustainable transportation infrastructure.

Smart Infrastructure

Developing intelligent transportation systems with IoT sensors, real-time monitoring, and data analytics for improved safety and efficiency.

Traffic Safety Analytics

Using advanced data analysis and AI to identify traffic safety patterns, predict risks, and develop preventive measures.

Sustainable Transportation

Researching eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient designs, and green transportation solutions to reduce environmental impact.

Connected Vehicles

Exploring vehicle-to-vehicle and vehicle-to-infrastructure communication technologies for enhanced safety and traffic management.

Infrastructure Resilience

Developing methods to enhance transportation infrastructure resilience against natural disasters and extreme weather events.

Autonomous Systems

Investigating the integration of autonomous vehicles with existing transportation infrastructure and safety implications.
