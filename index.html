About Us

The Safe and Agile Transportation Infrastructure (SATi) Lab at Kennesaw State University is dedicated to advancing transportation safety, efficiency, and sustainability through cutting-edge research and innovative technology solutions.

Research Excellence

Conducting high-impact research to address critical transportation challenges and improve infrastructure resilience.

Education

Training the next generation of transportation engineers and researchers through hands-on experience and mentorship.

Collaboration

Partnering with industry, government, and academic institutions to develop practical solutions for real-world transportation problems.
